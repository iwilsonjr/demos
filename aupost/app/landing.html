    Related FAQs

    How do I make a travel insurance claim?
    Click to expandClick to collapse

    Please visit make a claim for all the information you need to make a travel insurance claim.

    Are children covered under your travel insurance policies?
    Click to expandClick to collapse

    Yes, children are covered under your travel insurance policy for no additional charge, as long as they're not in full time employment, are less than 21 years of age and are travelling with you on the journey.

    When does my travel insurance policy start?
    Click to expandClick to collapse

    Cover for cancellation fees and lost deposits will begin as soon as your travel insurance policy is purchased for select policies.*

    Cover for all other aspects of your policy will begin on the day of your departure noted on your Certificate of Insurance (start date).

    Please note: You must purchase your policy before you commence your journey. You can purchase a travel insurance policy up to 12 months prior to your departure date.

    * Not available on the International Basic plan.
