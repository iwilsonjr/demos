Existing Medical Conditions (EMC)

Any claim arising from medical conditions that you have when you buy your policy will not be paid unless you declare them to us and we agree to cover them. There may be an extra charge.

Each person that has a medical condition to declare will need to buy a separate policy. If more than one person has a medical condition to declare please click the "Change Details" button in quote summary.

Travel Insurance does not automatically cover all existing conditions. Please note: If you are travelling to Americas or Africa and have a heart or lung condition or reduced immunity, please select 'yes' to the second question below. For a list of conditions we automatically cover free of charge and for pregnancy cover click here. Please make sure you do not declare a condition that is listed in the automatically covered list as such cover is free. If you do this an additional premium not otherwise charged will be included. If you make a mistake you can contact us and ask for a refund of the relevant amount applied to that condition, otherwise please select Yes'.
