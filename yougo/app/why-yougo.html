Why youGo

You travel. You’re smart. You know it makes sense to get travel insurance, but only if it’s a good value. Congratulations! You’ve come to the right place. YouGo offers affordable travel insurance that’s tailored just for you. With YouGo, you know you’re getting the best travel insurance cover at the best price.

Products

Your search for the best Aussie travel insurance ends here. No matter what you’ve got planned, our budget travel insurance products will keep you travelling smart and safe.

youGo Domestic

Save money on travel insurance for your Australian holiday. YouGo Domestic gives you everything you need (and nothing you don’t) to protect your investment and yourself when you travel in-country. With affordable cover for trip cancellation, luggage and rental car insurance excess, you get quality cover and great value.

youGo International

Overseas travel can be expensive, but covering your trip isn’t, if you choose YouGo. We offer cheap travel insurance that is also top-quality. Perfect for backpackers, families on holiday (and a budget), and students, our international cover lets you travel safe and secure.

youGo Annual

Are you a frequent traveller? Do you like to pick up and go at a moment’s notice? Our annual travel insurance was designed just for you. With year-long, high-quality cover that travels with you, the annual policy saves you time and money, and gives you the freedom to travel anytime knowing that you’re protected.

youGo Adventure Cover

So your planning an adrenaline-filled trip, are you? Our Adventure cover is for you. Not only are you automatically covered for countless activities, from ice skating to scuba diving, but you can purchase additional cover for ski travel insurance, moped and motorcycling, and more.
